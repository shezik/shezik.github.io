I am literally tired of Chinese-style education.

First of all, before I begin, I love China, as if it really works. We all love formalism, heh.
Unlike the post linked above, the only things I hate about China are the GFW and the education policy.

The only purpose of education here is to trim or stretch every single person to the same height. They don’t care about psychological stuff and shit at all. We do have a course about how to bring the best out of you, but that is 100% bullshit.
You know people do have their advantages and shortcomings. If a guy, unfortunately, is just not good at academics, he is fucked up here. I'll tell you what, no matter how great this guy is in other aspects. However, if you point this out to a parent, he or she would just say, “Schools don’t exam what YOU are good at. Get him after-school tutoring classes.”
Once on Baidu Tieba I saw a guy trying to build a nuclear reactor in his backyard, then you know what, his family sent him to a mental hospital. Whether this story is true or not, it tells us something.
The reality does not change after all. Right here, children start a nonsense racing against his peers once they are born.
You’ll see many children going out of elementary school and then right into some dancing classes or whatever speciality developing classes.There’s a classic saying among parents: Don’t let your children lose at the starting line.

By the way, don’t tell me “the population is too large in China” and blah. I constantly hear that, and that’s not a good excuse. We’re even getting enough food with Yuan Longping’s innovation.

Students who study so hard that would even forget how to walk normally (there is a girl like this in our senior high school) are often praised. Still, isn’t there any advantage in a person except being able to study well?
I am tired out of this shit.

Fuck.
