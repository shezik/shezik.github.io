2020-01-08

I am literally tired of Chinese-style education.

First of all, before I begin, I love China, as if it really works. We all love formalism, heh.
Unlike the post linked above, the only things I hate about China is the GFW and the education policy.

The only purpose of education here is to trim or stretch every single person to the same height. They don’t care about psychological stuff at all. We do have a course telling you how to bring the best out of you, but that is pure shit.
You know people do have their advantages and shortcomings. If a guy, unfortunately, is just not good at academics, then he is born fucked here, no matter how great this guy is in other aspects. However, if you point this out to a parent, he or she would just say, “Schools don’t exam what YOU are good at. Get him after-school tutoring classes.”
Once on Baidu Tieba I saw a guy trying to build a nuclear reactor in his backyard, then I’ll tell you what, his family sent him to a mental hospital. Whether this story is true or not, it tells us something.
The reality does not change after all. Right here, children start a nonsense racing against his peers once they are born.
You’ll see many children going out of elementary school and then right into some dancing classes or whatever speciality developing classes.There’s a classic saying among parents: Don’t let your children lose at the starting line.

By the way, don’t tell me “the population is too large in China” and blah. I constantly hear that, and that’s not a good excuse. We’re even getting enough food with Yuan Longping’s innovation.
Students who study so hard that would even forget how to walk properly (a girl in our senior high school) are often praised. Still, isn’t there any advantage in a person expect being able to study well?
I am tired out of this shit.

Come later for more. That’s it for today.
